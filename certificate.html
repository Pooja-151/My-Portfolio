I completed a eight-week Summer Internship on AI-Powered Web Application Development at IGDTUW, where I developed a strong foundation in full-stack development and gained exposure to integrating artificial intelligence (AI) into real-world applications. The internship began with strengthening my skills in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which helped me understand how to design responsive and user-friendly interfaces. Working under the guidance of mentors and collaborating with peers, I improved my problem-solving, research, and teamwork skills. I also learned how to approach development from an end-to-end perspective, including requirement gathering, planning, coding, and testing. Although I have not yet built a project based on this internship, I now have the confidence and technical knowledge to create applications like AI-based recommendation systems or intelligent chatbots. This internship was a transformative step in my journey as a Computer Science Engineering student, preparing me for future projects, hackathons, and career opportunities.
